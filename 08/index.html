<div class="textcontainer"> <br></br> <h3>Week 8: Composites</h3> <p class = "margin"></p> We encountered our second challenge in the building process. The first challenge was connecting the sidewalls at the bow. The second challenge is... gunwales! While fitting the gunwales prior to their final installation, many of them snapped due to the massiveness and impressive curvature of our 14ft yacht. We explored multiple careful strategies to install the gunwales, but they (plural) kept snapping. Towards the end of class last week, Johnna, our team's chemist, mixed up a fresh batch of epoxy to conjoin the broken gunwales. Hopefully, the gunwales are stronger than ever and ready to perform when we resume building this week. If not, we will explore other ideas and still deliver a flawless yacht as a final product. Just how we figured out and overcame the first challenge, we will do the same for the second.
